Get to know me!

Terve! I'm Ella Elomaa, a masters student at Tampere University, majoring in databases and information retrieval. From my very first SQL lecture I fell in love with the subject, to much chagrin of my friends - alas, databases is not the most mediagenic of IT fields. Obviously, they have poor taste.

Often I've been described as a positive energy in any group project, keeping the spirits high even when deadlines are looming and things seem dire. I get things done, but I always try to look on the bright side of things at the same time. I keep to deadlines, lend a helping hand, and I'm never afraid to ask questions to better my skills. So if you're looking for a reliable, cheery teammate, look no further!
